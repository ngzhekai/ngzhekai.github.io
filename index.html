About Me

こんにちは！Ng Zhe Kaiと申します、 Gilbert って呼んでもいいよ〜

Hey there! I'm Zhe Kai, aka Gilbert.

I'm currently a student at Multimedia University, who is pursuing an undergraduate Bachelor's Degree in Data Communication & Networking. However, deep down in my heart, I have a passion for the Linux system (due to less bloat...), so I moved to Linux.

During my free time, I will listen to music, practise my Japanese, and maybe edit my dotfiles. I sometimes get motivated to learn new skills (i.e. programming languages, video-editing). Speaking of my hobby, I really do loves to execute the command sudo pacman -Syu, I would have to say that I execute this command very frequently.

In terms of my goal, I have always wanted to build a website for myself. However, due to my lack of expertise in the field of Web Development, I enrolled myself in a Web Development course offered by Coursera. I am so grateful I managed to build my very first website.
